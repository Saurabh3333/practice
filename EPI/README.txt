These are my solutions to problems from Elements of Programming Interviews
by Aziz, Prakash, and Lee.

The solutions are named CC/soln_CC_NNN_{name}..., where CC is the
chapter number, NNN is the problem number within the chapter, and
{description} is a short name for the problem solved.
